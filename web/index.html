Sticky Prompter

A voice-controlled sticky-note teleprompter that floats on top of your video calls and recordings. It listens as you speak and follows along — no scrolling, no clicking.

⚠️ Voice tracking needs Chrome (or Edge) — this browser doesn't support the Web Speech API. Timed auto-scroll will still work.

How to use it over a video call: click the pop-out button (⧉) on the note — it becomes an always-on-top mini window that floats over Zoom, Meet, or anything else. Park it right under your camera.

Voice tracking: hit the mic, then just read. Words dim as you say them and the highlight tracks your place — pause, ad-lib, or skip a sentence and it catches up. Click any word to jump there.

Keys: space mic on/off · ←/→ nudge position · R restart · E edit script
