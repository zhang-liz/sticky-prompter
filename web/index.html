Sticky Prompter

A voice-controlled sticky note that floats over your video calls and recordings. It listens as you speak and follows along — no scrolling, no clicking.

⚠️ Voice tracking needs Chrome (or Edge) — this browser doesn't support the Web Speech API. Timed auto-scroll will still work.

Float it over your call

Click the pop-out button on the note — it becomes an always-on-top mini window that floats over Zoom, Meet, or anything else. Park it right under your camera.

Voice tracking

Hit the mic, then just read. Words dim as you say them — pause, ad-lib, or skip a sentence and it catches up. Click any word to jump there.
